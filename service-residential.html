SIGNS THAT INDICATE A POOL LEAK

Pool leaks affect the water quality and water level in your swimming pool. In addition to residential pool care and pool cleaning, Oshin Pools also offers pool leak detection and pool repair; you can reach us at +91 9995844533, +91 9072334254

  • Normal evaporation tends to cause about 1/4” of water loss per day. If you notice a dropping water level that is greater than this amount, your pool may have a leak.
  • Pool leaks can cause water to drain into the surrounding area. This may cause pool decking to shift, crack, or rot, and can also affect nearby landscaping.
  • Water loss affects the efficacy of your pool chemicals. A leak may cause the water to feel uncomfortable or algae to grow quickly even after treatment.
