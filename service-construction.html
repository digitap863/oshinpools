Construction Services

Based in Cochin, Kerala, Oshin Pools are Pool Contractors who can design and construct the pool of your dreams. Our Founders have about 20 years of experience in the field of design & construction of swimming pools, water bodies, internal water features, fountains etc.

Our dedication to quality service, innovation, custom design capability and timely construction service led Oshin Pools to emerge as a reliable Swimming Pool Contractor in Kerala.

Our commitment to innovation, efficiency and prompt after sales support are hallmarks of our services which ensure scores of satisfied loyal customers. At Oshin Pools, we understand that our brand's success is mainly dependent upon the abilities of our team. The value Oshin Pools places on expertise, innovative techniques and dedication has allowed us to undertake and complete a number of aquatic projects of various Sizes & Shapes.

In a short span of time, Oshin Pools has developed a trustworthy and dependable reputation in the industry. This has allowed us to cultivate and sustain professional partnerships, thereby allowing us to bring in the best possible solution and customized perspective to every project. Here at Oshin Pools, we recognize that no Swimming Pool, no fountain, and certainly no home, is "one-size-fits-all". Hence we are available to provide customized services to our esteemed customers.

Oshin Pools is an experienced Commercial / Residential Pool Contractor to construct pools of any Size & Shape in Kerala and neighbouring states.

Our construction crews are highly skilled craftsmen who are second to none in experience and are well-versed in all types of aquatic construction. Our design and construction management team will ensure the highest level of quality in our work, and the timely completion of your Swimming Pool.

RENOVATION AND RESTORATION

Our design and construction professionals will be there during every step of your restoration project. They will help you to bring your vision into a beautiful and functional design and will make it a reality. We will take your entire project from concept through 100% turnkey construction, including the construction of the pool deck/sidewalks and all related works.
