About Us

OSHIN POOLS was established with the goal of providing quality products and services to the absolute satisfaction of our esteemed customers. OSHIN POOLS is a rapidly growing company, having the wholehearted support of our customers, spread across Kerala and the neighbouring states.

We offer complete solutions in the following areas:

01. Swimming Pool, Jacuzzi & Spa Filtration Systems
02. Indoor & Outdoor Fountains & Water Feature Systems
03. Water Treatment Plants
04. Steam Bath and Sauna Bath
05. Operation & Maintenance Contracts
06. Supply of Pool Equipment, Accessories, Consumables & Pool Chemicals

We design, develop and construct customized, cost effective products and provide services to suit individual customer needs. We not only take care of creativity but also ensure the perfect and timely execution of projects we undertake. With many years of cumulative experience and expertise, our services cover the full range of products, equipment, accessories and chemicals related to the above fields. OSHIN POOLS associates with the most reputed and innovative companies who are leaders in their respective areas. With our vast experience, we aim to place safety and quality first, in everything we do. We have a dedicated team of professionals comprising of design engineers and technicians who are committed to implement efficient and cost-effective methods to supply the best products at all times. Our strength lies in having a thorough knowledge of pool construction/ maintenance, filtration, underwater illumination and the ability to understand the needs of our clients.

Leading by example

Pool Services have long been renowned as the leading Swimming Pool Company throughout Surrey, Sussex and the South East leading the charge in the Industries best practices as well as being at the forefront of the latest technological advancements.
